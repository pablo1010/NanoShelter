The World’s First Pocket-Sized, Waterproof Pop-Up Tent

The Problem: Nature is unpredictable. When the rain hits or wind howls, traditional tents are slow, heavy, and cumbersome. Urban travelers and outdoor lovers alike need a quick, compact solution.

How It Started: I was out with a couple of friends on the motorbike. It was forecasted to rain but not in the location we were travelling, so off we went with just the clothes we were wearing. We got to the destination and the heavens opened up with unbelievably loads of rain, we had no protection from the elements and carrying a tent with us on the go wasn't practical as they are bulky and heavy and out popped the idea. Wouldn't it be great if we had something small and compact that we could fit into our jackets or our back packs and if and when needed just deploy in a matter of seconds and in we go and dry we would be.

The Solution: NanoShelter is a revolutionary, patent-pending micro-tent that folds into your pocket and deploys in under 10 seconds. 100% waterproof, ultralight, and built for modern on-the-go protection.
