NanoShelter™

The World’s First Pocket-Sized, Waterproof Pop-Up Tent

The Problem
Nature is unpredictable. When the rain hits or wind howls, traditional tents are slow, heavy, and cumbersome. Urban travelers and outdoor lovers alike need a quick, compact solution.

How the idea came about
I was out with a couple of friends on the motorbike. It was forecasted to rain but not in the location we were travelling, so off we went with just the clothes we were wearing. We got to the destination and the heavens opened up with unbelievably loads of rain, we had no protection from the elements and carrying a tent with us on the go wasn't practical as they are bulky and heavy and out popped the idea...

The Solution: NanoShelter™
NanoShelter is a revolutionary, patent-pending micro-tent that folds into your pocket and deploys in under 10 seconds. 100% waterproof, ultralight, and built for modern on-the-go protection.

Why Choose NanoShelter™?

  • 100% Waterproof Protection: Stay dry in any weather.
  • Pocket-Sized Convenience: Fits easily in your jacket or cargo pocket.
  • 3-Second Setup: Pops up instantly. No poles. No stress.
  • All-Terrain Utility: Use it anywhere — forest, fields, roadside.
  • Ultra Lightweight & Durable: Built tough, carried light.
  • Perfect for: Travel, hikes, festivals, emergencies & more.
